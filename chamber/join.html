Membership Levels

NP Membership Level

Designed for non-profit organizations and educational institutions.

Non-Profit Membership (NP)

No membership fee. Access to community networking, listing in the business directory, and invitation to public chamber meetings.

Bronze Membership

Includes basic business directory listing, access to networking events, and discounted rates for chamber workshops.

Silver Membership

Includes enhanced directory listing, priority event registration, mid-level advertising options, and social media spotlight options.

Gold Membership

Full suite of benefits, premier homepage spotlight feature, free entry to major chamber networking events, and logo placement on promotional materials.
